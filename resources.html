üõ°Ô∏è Safety Tips & Planning

Create a Safety Plan

Develop a personalized plan for staying safe at home, work, and in public. Include emergency contacts, safe places to go, and important documents to keep ready.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong or unsafe, trust your gut feelings. Your instincts are often right, and it's better to be safe than sorry.

Document Everything

Keep records of incidents, including photos, messages, and witness information. Store copies in a safe place away from your home.

Build a Support Network

Connect with trusted friends, family, and professionals who can help. Having people you can rely on is crucial for your safety and wellbeing.

Secure Your Technology

Protect your devices and online accounts from unauthorized access. Change passwords regularly and be aware of tracking apps.
